  • "Cherishing the craft of India, we are known for our gifting ideas and festive, office and home decor."

  • We have 2 trademarks registered as Gogalgai and Gogalgai Toys, issued by the Government of India, giving us the leverage to enter the market with a variety of unique, hand-made wooden products.
  • Having filed the International Product Patents for innovative products developed indigenously, we have also been awarded the membership of Export Promotion Council for Handicrafts (EPCH) as well as the membership of Sports Goods Export Promotion Council (SGEPC).
  • Established in 2014, we were invited to participate in the Rashtrapati Bhavan's Festival of Innovation- 8th edition on 7th March 2015 and to meet the President of India then, Mr. Pranab Mukherjee. We were also invited to participate in India International Trade Fair (IITF), New Delhi in 2016 and the International Toy Fair Nuremberg, Germany, in 2018.
